Westwood League of Developers

Using modern technologies to make life easier

The Westwood League of Developers (WeLD) is a group of aspiring developers at Westwood High School who envision new applications, share their ideas, and gather and join teams to see their ideas and hard work bear fruit. We aim to improve student life and develop our own collaborative and technical skills.

Projects

  • QuickHAC: a Chrome extension to check your grades
  • Schedule Comparinator: an annual comparator for student schedules

Join us!

Do you like programming? Do you have an idea that could make your life easier? Do you want to hang out with other programmers? We meet the first Monday afternoon of each month in Mr. Mickel's room (E2303). We'd love to have you join our ranks!

Previous programming experience is strongly recommended but not re­quired. However, wanting to work with computers is required.
