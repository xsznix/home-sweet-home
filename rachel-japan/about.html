Hi! I'm Rachel.

This summer I will be going to Tohoku, Japan along with 6 other people from Austin Chinese Church with Japanese Evangelical Missionary Society (JEMS). Tohoku is in northern Japan and was heavily impacted by the March 11 tsunami in 2011. It has been two years and the people there still desperately need physical, emotional, and most importantly, spiritual help. My team is going to Minamisanriku, where we will be partnering with SOLA and helping with children’s ministry. This will be my first missions trip and I am beyond excited to see what God has in store for me and the people of Japan!

Please consider supporting me to make this trip possible.

Pray for me.

Pray that God will provide friendships with key people and open their hearts to the gospel message. Pray for boldness and courage as I live out Christ’s love in faith.
