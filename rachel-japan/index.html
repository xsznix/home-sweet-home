Hi! I'm Rachel.

This summer I will be going to Tohoku, Japan along with 6 other people from Austin Chinese Church with Japanese Evangelical Missionary Society (JEMS). Tohoku is in northern Japan and was heavily impacted by the March 11 tsunami in 2011. It has been two years and the people there still desperately need physical, emotional, and most importantly, spiritual help. My team is going to Minamisanriku, where we will be partnering with SOLA and helping with children’s ministry. This will be my first missions trip and I am beyond excited to see what God has in store for me and the people of Japan!

In order for this trip to happen, I need your partnership. First, please consider being one of my prayer partners. Pray that God will provide friendships with key people and open their hearts to the gospel message. Pray for boldness and courage as I live out Christ’s love in faith.

Second, prayerfully consider being a financial supporter as well. My goal is to raise $2,900 by July 15th to cover the cost of the trip. You can peruse this wonderfully designed website and email me what you would like order. The prices next to each item are suggestions; please donate however much or little you would like—all proceeds go towards funding this trip. I will arrange for you to receive the item(s) you ordered in a timely manner.

If nothing on this site interests you, you can still help by giving online! First, go to the secured JEMS online donation page. Next, under Categories and Funds please select “Japan Short-term Missions.” A subcategory slot will appear and under that select “Austin Chinese Church Japan Summer Missions 2013.” Go ahead and fill out the rest of the donation page and JEMS will properly credit the account.

Thank you so much for visiting this site and thank you in advance for your support in any form. I trust that God will provide for everything and his glory will be made known to the nations.

Love,
Rachel
