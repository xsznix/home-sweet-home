IB WAS Word 2007 Template

Microsoft Word's formatting can be confusing sometimes. Fortunately, using LaTeX to write your paper can help you save a lot of trouble getting everything correct. But if that's not your thing, there's a Word template, too.

Click here to download the template for Microsoft Word 2007 or later.

Quirks

  • Bad typography is bad. Make sure that when copying and pasting, you match the destination style instead of the source style.
  • Please don't use Times New Roman. Ever.
