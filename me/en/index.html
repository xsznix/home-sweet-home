Stuff I've Programmed

QuickHAC

QuickHAC is a covenient alternative interface to the gradebook website used by my former high school district, implemented as a Chrome extension. It aims to provide a superior user experience by organizing information in a layout that is intuitive, informative, and aesthetically pleasing. It is on the Chrome Web Store. The grades shown in the screenshot at the right are not anybody's real grades and are for demonstration purposes only.

Schedule Comparinator

Schedule Comparinator is an annual web app for middle and high school students to find out who's in their classes before the first class day. Students enter in their schedules and instantly see who else is in the classes that they're in. Schedule Comparinator runs from mid-August to early September.

Binaural Beats

The most flexible binaural beats generator on the web (that I know of), featuring a skeuomorphic, DAW plugin-like control panel and five types of noise generation. Uses the HTML 5 Web Audio API.

Virtual TR707

A straightforward, keyboard-enabled, plugin-free and (mostly) lag-free online drum kit in JavaScript using the HTML 5 Audio API. Includes the ability to record riffs to plaintext and play them back.
